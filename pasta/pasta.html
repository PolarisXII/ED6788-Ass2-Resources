Spaghetti Bolognese

Spaghetti bolognese is a type of pasta from Italy.

It tastes delicious and is very easy to make.

I like to make the sauce with beef mince and tomatos, and top it off with parmasan cheese and parsley.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Tomato
  • Parsley
